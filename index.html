MovieRec

Welcome to our study! Our team has developed a movie recommender system called MovieRec that generates recommendations of movies for you to watch. The system also generates explanations for why it recommended a paticular movie to you.

In this study, you will be asked to rate different movie recommendation explanations generated by the system, as well as the actual movie recommendations produced by the system for you, given a specific scenario.

The first part of the study will ask you questions about yourself and how you approach movies. Our MovieRec system will use the information to generate the movie recommendations and explanations for you to rate in the later part of the study.


Responses will be checked for quality. We reserve the right to not provide compensation to workers who provide responses that do not match quality criteria, such as one-word answers or all the same ratings given to all questions. At the end of the survey, you will receive a code to paste into the box below to receive credit for taking our survey.
